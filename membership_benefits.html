Membership Benefits Home » Membership Area » Membership Benefits

We need to join Alumni Association to strengthen ourselves so that we can be together for ourselves in several ways:

  • We get chance to re-live our good times together, remembering back the great institute that gives us the reason of who and where we are today

  • We get chance to interact with our old mates with whom we shared some wonderful memories of life, the warmth of which will always remain with us forever.

  • We get chance to seek support and guidance from our alumni group, which consists of people in every sphere of life – science, technology, engineering, business, politics, civil services, public sectors, etc. – having experience up to 50 years with them

  • We get chance to give guidance and support to people in our alumni group, which in turn is way to pay-back to our great institute of knowledge and learning

  • We get chance to reach out and connect to people in our alumni group spread all over the country and across the world at various positions and levels

  • We get chance to derive benefits from the market by the virtue of our numbers in many aspects of our lives

  • We get chance to interact with a large alumni group to find answer to our queries on all aspects of life – children, education, work, business, sourcing, travel, etc.

  • We get chance to be with people from our alumni group, with whom we share so much in common even by just belonging to the same institute for an important period of our life

  • We get chance to come together for some common cause we feel for or care for – welfare of our hostel mess workers who took care for us like our family when we were away from our own family, scholarship for bright children who get admission into our institute but are unable to pay for their education, etc.

That is how “We get chance to come together for ourselves.”
