Mission Home » About us » Mission

The society shall be for the benefit of all ex-students of National Institute of Technology, formerly known as, Regional Engineering College located at Kurukshetra, Haryana. The main objects for which the society is established are:

  1. To build an active, lifelong relationship between the college / institute and its former students.

  2. To foster a spirit of mutual help and goodwill among the society in general, and the members of the Association in particular, thereby, promoting communal harmony and national integration.

  3. To organize welfare meets (get-togethers) and functions for interaction of members to promote social brotherhood amongst the members.

  4. To undertake to set-up public libraries, reading-rooms audio visual centers for the advancements of education and entertainment of the members.

  5. To give scholarships, prizes, loans, financial assistance and / or other assistance to the needy, deserving and / or poor students.

  6. To provide medical, surgical relief.

  7. To acquire and purchase, take on lease or rent, hire or by way of any donation and hold movable and / or immovable properties that may be deemed necessary for attainment of the objects of the society;

  8. To raise funds through grants, donations, subscriptions, loans and contributions in cash or in kind for the fulfillment of all or any of the objects of the society.

  9. To assist the college / institute in organizing events / functions.

  10. To undertake all such other lawful acts, deeds or things including functions, Sports and Cultural activities as are incidental or conducive to the attainment of any or all of the above objectives.

“All the incomes, earnings, movable properties of the society shall be solely utilized and applied towards the promotion of its aims and objects only as set forth in the Memorandum of Association and no profit on thereof shall be paid or transferred directly or indirectly by way of dividends, bonus, profits or in any manner whatsoever to the present or past Members of the society or to any person claiming through anyone or more of the present or past members. No member of the society shall have any movable or immovable properties of the society or make any profits whatsoever, by virtue of this membership”.
